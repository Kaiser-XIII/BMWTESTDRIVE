About The 7th generation 5 series

An icon is reborn

In this seventh generation business saloon, the iconic kidney grille and headlights are directly linked for the first time ever. Adding to this sportier look is the low roof line sloping to the rear; this ensures that the muscular body and the broad rear still enjoy a distinguished and sophisticated coupé-like silhouette.
